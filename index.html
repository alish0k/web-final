Welcome to our platform dedicated to exploring the crucial relationship between fast food and health. In an era of convenience, fast food has become an integral part of modern lifestyles. However, it's essential to delve into the profound effects these quick bites can have on our well-being.

Our mission

At NutriBalance, we are on a mission to empower individuals and communities to make informed, healthier choices in their daily eating habits. We believe that everyone deserves access to accurate and engaging information about the impact of fast food on human health.

Our story

Founded in 2023 by Olga Luzinskaya a nutrition enthusiast with a background in “Eurasia” medical center. NutriBalance was born out of a deep concern for the rising health issues associated with the prevalence of fast food in our modern lifestyles. Having personally witnessed the transformative power of adopting a healthier diet, Olga Luzinskaya envisioned a platform that could inspire positive change on a broader scale.
